Meatballs with Beer

Do you currently have a craving for beer and meatballs? If so then you're in luck!

Here's the recipie

Ingredients

  • Energy
  • Gas in your car
  • $20 (preferably broken into $5 notes)
  • Frozen meatballs
  • A six pack of your fav Beer
  • Preperation time = 30 - 40 mins

The Steps

  1. Get up off of the couch and drive to the nearest grocery store
  2. Go to the frozen food isle and locate and select your favorite frozen meatball brand
  3. Walk to the beer section (if you don't live in a state that allows beer to be sold in grocery stores, add anout 20 - 30 mins to your prep time to go pick some up)
  4. Pick up a six pack of your fav beer
  5. Head to the cashier and pay for beer and meatballs with your cash
  6. Head home and placee meatballs in a microwave or oven safe container and floow instructions onthe pack
  7. Open one beer and enjoy while meatballs cooks
  8. when meatballs are ready enjoy them with the second beer
